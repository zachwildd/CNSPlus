// FILE NOTES

Vanth was the ninth augment to walk out of the Burning Ward alive, and the only one who walked out smiling. The corporate labs spliced feline reflexes and a heat-core into a body they expected to obey. They miscalculated the smile.

The pearl plating is grafted, not worn — fused to skin and bone, threaded with the red linework of an experimental cooling lattice that glows when the heat-core spins up. When Vanth ignites, the flame doesn't burn outward by accident. It is aimed.

Now Vanth runs the lower sectors as something between a folk hero and a fire alarm: freeing the wards the labs still operate, one collapsed roof at a time. The bounty grows monthly. So does the legend.

Last sighting: Burning Ward 12 — structure since condemned.
